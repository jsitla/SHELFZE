# Privacy Policy for Shelfze

**Last Updated: October 29, 2025**

## Introduction

Welcome to Shelfze. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy while providing a valuable food management service.

**By using Shelfze, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy.**

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly

**Food Item Data:**
- Food names (scanned or manually entered)
- Expiration dates
- Quantities
- Storage locations
- Categories
- Custom notes

**User Preferences:**
- Language selection
- App settings
- Recipe preferences

**Images:**
- Photos of food items and product labels you capture using the camera
- These images are processed but not permanently stored by us

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

**Device Information:**
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- App version
- Unique device identifier (anonymous)

**Usage Data:**
- Features used within the App
- Frequency of use
- Scan success/failure rates
- Recipe generation requests
- Error logs and crash reports

**Authentication Data:**
- Anonymous user ID (generated by Firebase Authentication)
- Account creation date
- Last login timestamp

**Communication Data:**
- Push notification tokens (for expiry alerts)
- Notification preferences
- Alert history

### 1.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:
- Your real name, email, or phone number (unless you contact support)
- Payment information (app is currently free)
- Contacts or address book
- Location data
- Health data or biometric information
- Social media profiles

### 1.3 Camera Access

The App requires camera permission to:
- Scan food product barcodes and labels
- Capture images of expiration dates
- Take photos of food items for identification
- Record video for real-time scanning (mobile devices only)

**Camera images are:**
- Processed temporarily for AI analysis
- Sent to Google Cloud Vision API and Gemini AI for processing
- NOT stored permanently on our servers
- Deleted after processing is complete

## 2. How We Use Your Information

### 2.1 Primary Purposes

We use your information to:
- **Provide Core Services:**
  - Scan and identify food items using AI
  - Detect expiration dates from images
  - Store your pantry inventory
  - Generate personalized recipe suggestions
  - Track expiring items and send notifications

- **Improve App Functionality:**
  - Fix bugs and errors
  - Optimize AI accuracy
  - Enhance user experience
  - Develop new features

### 2.2 Product Improvement and AI Training

**IMPORTANT: We may use your data to improve our services and AI models.**

Specifically, we may use:
- **Anonymized scan data** to improve food recognition accuracy
- **Recipe generation patterns** to enhance AI recommendations
- **Usage statistics** to identify popular features and pain points
- **Error logs** to fix bugs and improve stability
- **Aggregated data** for analytics and trend analysis

**Data used for improvement is:**
- Anonymized and de-identified
- Not linked to your personal identity
- Aggregated with data from other users
- Used solely for improving Shelfze services

**You cannot opt out of this data usage**, as it is essential to maintaining and improving the App. If you do not agree, please do not use Shelfze.

### 2.3 Analytics and Performance

We use Firebase Analytics to:
- Monitor app performance
- Track feature usage
- Identify crash patterns
- Understand user behavior
- Measure engagement

### 3.4 What We DON'T Share

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Where Your Data is Stored

Your data is stored in:
- **Google Cloud Firestore** (Firebase) - Primary database
- **Firebase Authentication** - User authentication
- **Firebase Cloud Functions** - Server-side processing

Data is stored on Google's servers, which may be located in various regions worldwide.

### 4.2 Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as you use the App:
- **Food inventory data:** Until you delete items or delete your account
- **Usage analytics:** Up to 14 months (Firebase default)
- **Crash reports:** Up to 90 days
- **Temporary images:** Deleted immediately after AI processing

### 4.3 Security Measures

We implement security measures including:
- **HTTPS encryption** for all data transmission
- **Firebase Authentication** with secure anonymous login
- **User-specific data isolation** (you can only access your own data)
- **Firestore Security Rules** to prevent unauthorized access
- **Regular security audits** and updates

**However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.** We cannot guarantee absolute security.

### 4.4 Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach affecting your information:
- We will investigate and assess the breach within 72 hours
- Affected users will be notified via in-app notification (if possible)
- We will report breaches to relevant authorities as required by law
- We will take immediate steps to secure data and prevent future breaches

**Given anonymous authentication, identifying affected users may be difficult.** We recommend monitoring the App for security updates.
