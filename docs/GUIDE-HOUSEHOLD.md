## Creating a Household

### Steps
1. Open **Profile** (tap icon in Pantry screen)
2. Scroll to **Household** section
3. Tap **Create Household**
4. Enter a household name (e.g., "Smith Family")
5. Tap **Create**
6. You're now the **household owner**!

### After Creating
- Your personal pantry becomes the household pantry
- An **invite code** is generated (6 characters)
- Share this code with family members

---

## Joining a Household

### Steps
1. Get the invite code from the household owner
2. Open **Profile** â†’ **Household**
3. Tap **Join Household**
4. Enter the 6-character code
5. Tap **Join**

### After Joining
- You now see the shared household pantry
- Your scans add items to the shared pantry
- You use credits from the shared pool

## Household Credits

Credits are shared across all household members:

| Tier | Scans/Month | Recipes/Month |
|------|-------------|---------------|
| Free Household | 30 | 30 |
| Premium Household | 500 | 500 |

### Premium Upgrade
If **any** member has Premium, the whole household gets Premium credits!

## Leaving a Household

### Steps
1. Go to Profile â†’ Household
2. Tap **Leave Household**
3. Confirm your decision

### What Happens
- You return to your personal pantry
- Your archived credits are restored
- **7-day cooldown** before joining another household

### Cooldown Period
After leaving, you must wait 7 days before joining or creating a new household. This prevents credit abuse.

---

## For Household Owners

### Managing the Household
As the owner, you:
- Created the household
- Can share the invite code
- Are the primary member

### If Owner Leaves
Currently, leaving as owner will disband the household. All members return to personal pantries.

---

## Credit Archiving

### When You Join
Your personal credits are saved (archived) and you start using household credits.

### When You Leave
Your archived personal credits are restored, so you don't lose anything!

## Tips

- **Family coordination** - Designate who adds grocery scans
- **Premium value** - One Premium member benefits everyone
- **Check before buying** - Anyone can check the shared pantry
- **Update quantities** - When items are used, update the pantry

## FAQ

**Q: Can I be in multiple households?**  
A: No, you can only be in one household at a time.

**Q: What happens to my items when I join?**  
A: Your personal pantry is paused. When you leave, it's restored.

**Q: Can members delete items?**  
A: Yes, all members can add, edit, and delete items.

**Q: How many members can join?**  
A: No limit on household size.
