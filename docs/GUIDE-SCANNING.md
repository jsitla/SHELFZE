## Photo Mode (Default)

### How to Scan
1. Open the **Scanner** tab
2. Position your camera to show the food item or label
3. Tap the **white capture button**
4. Wait 2-3 seconds for AI processing
5. Review detected items in the popup
6. Tap **Save All** or edit individual items

### Tips for Best Results
- âœ… Good lighting - use the flashlight button if needed
- âœ… Hold steady - avoid blurry photos
- âœ… Show expiry date clearly - AI reads dates automatically
- âœ… Scan multiple items at once - AI detects several products
- âœ… Include product labels for better recognition

### What AI Detects
- Product name (e.g., "Whole Milk", "Greek Yogurt")
- Category (Dairy, Meat, Vegetables, etc.)
- Expiry date (various formats: DD/MM/YYYY, Best Before, etc.)
- Quantity estimate
- Confidence score (%)
