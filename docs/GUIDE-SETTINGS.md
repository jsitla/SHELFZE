## Account Management

### Account Types
- **Anonymous** - Quick start, no credentials
- **Email Account** - Email + password
- **Google Account** - Sign in with Google
- **Apple Account** - Sign in with Apple (iOS)

### Upgrading from Anonymous
1. Go to Profile
2. Tap **Create Account**
3. Choose sign-in method
4. Your data is preserved!

## Sign Out

### Steps
1. Scroll to bottom of Profile
2. Tap **Sign Out**
3. Confirm

### What Happens
- You're logged out
- If anonymous: Data remains on device
- If signed in: Data synced to cloud
