## Viewing a Recipe

Tap any recipe card to see full details:

### Recipe Information
- **Ingredients** - With exact quantities
- **Instructions** - Step-by-step guide
- **Timing** - Prep time, cook time, total time
- **Servings** - Portion size
- **Difficulty** - Easy, Medium, Hard
- **Cuisine** - Italian, Mexican, etc.
- **Chef's Tips** - Pro advice for best results

### Adjusting Servings
Use the +/- buttons to scale ingredients for more or fewer servings.

## Chef's Table (Custom Recipes)

Create recipes for specific cravings!

### How to Use
1. Tap **Chef's Table** button
2. Describe what you want:
   - "Spicy chicken tacos"
   - "Quick pasta under 20 minutes"
   - "Healthy vegetarian dinner"
3. Tap **Generate**
4. AI creates a custom recipe from your pantry

### Tips for Best Results
- Be specific about what you want
- Mention dietary preferences
- Include time constraints if any
