## Subscription Plans

### Monthly
- Billed every month
- Cancel anytime
- Best for trying Premium

### Annual
- Billed once per year
- **Save up to 40%** compared to monthly
- Best value for regular users

## How to Subscribe

### Steps
1. Open **Profile** (icon in Pantry screen)
2. Tap **Upgrade to Premium**
3. Choose Monthly or Annual plan
4. Complete payment via App Store / Google Play
5. Premium activates immediately!

### Payment
- Processed securely by Apple or Google
- We never see your credit card details
- Cancel anytime in your device settings
